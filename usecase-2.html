Why HEVC and VP9?

Advanced codecs provides same or better quality at lower bitrate.

  • HEVC provides upto 50% savings
  • VP9 provides ~30% savings
HEVC and VP9 device support

In recent years codecs are widely adopted across the industry

  • HEVC support on iOS 11+
  • HEVC support on Mac OS High Sierra
  • Android 5.0+
  • VP9 standard on Chrome
